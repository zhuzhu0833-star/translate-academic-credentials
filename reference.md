## Grade scales

### Percentage (百分制)
Keep numeric scores as-is: `85`, `92.5`.

### Five-level (五级制)

| 中文 | English (common) |
|------|------------------|
| 优 | Excellent |
| 良 | Good |
| 中 | Satisfactory / Average |
| 及格 | Pass |
| 不及格 | Fail |

### Four-level (四级制)

| 中文 | English |
|------|---------|
| 优 | Excellent |
| 良 | Good |
| 及格 | Pass |

## Notes on institution names

Prefer official English names. Common examples:

| 中文 | Official English |
|------|------------------|
| 北京大学 | Peking University |
| 清华大学 | Tsinghua University |
| 复旦大学 | Fudan University |
| 上海交通大学 | Shanghai Jiao Tong University |
| 浙江大学 | Zhejiang University |
| 中国人民大学 | Renmin University of China |

When uncertain, search for the institution's official English name on its website or MOE records before translating.
